# TRV-SHIELD Roadmap

## Mission

To build a prevention-first environmental resilience and interception framework capable of protecting public health, ecosystems, and coastal infrastructure throughout the Tijuana River Valley region.

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:

> Dignity Over Power
