# TRV-SHIELD Architecture

## Mission

TRV-SHIELD is a protective environmental interception and resilience architecture for the Tijuana River Valley.

Its purpose is to prevent contaminated surge flows from becoming uncontrolled public-health, ecological, and coastal infrastructure failures.

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:

> Dignity Over Power
