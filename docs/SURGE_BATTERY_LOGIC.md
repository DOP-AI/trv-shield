# Surge Battery Logic

## Purpose

One of the central principles of TRV-SHIELD is that uncontrolled surge conditions create cascading environmental failure.

Traditional wastewater and flood infrastructure often assumes stable operating conditions.

The Tijuana River Valley does not operate under stable conditions.

It operates under:

- extreme surge variability
- storm escalation
- sediment loading
- cross-border contamination spikes
- infrastructure stress
- pump failures
- overflow conditions
- unpredictable inflow surges

TRV-SHIELD addresses this using a concept called:

> Surge Battery Logic

---

# Core Concept

A surge battery is temporary environmental stabilization capacity.

Its purpose is to absorb instability before collapse propagates downstream.

Instead of allowing sudden contamination surges to overwhelm treatment systems, coastlines, estuaries, or communities, the system temporarily stores and stabilizes excess flow.
