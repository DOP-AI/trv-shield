# Flood Survival Doctrine

## Mission

TRV-SHIELD is designed for environments where instability is expected.

Flooding, surge events, infrastructure overload, contamination spikes, and severe weather are not considered rare anomalies within the Tijuana River Valley region.

They are expected operational realities.

Because of this, TRV-SHIELD follows a foundational principle:

> Infrastructure must remain survivable during instability.

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:

> Dignity Over Power

---

# Core Philosophy

Traditional infrastructure often assumes stable operating conditions.

TRV-SHIELD assumes:

- surge conditions will occur
- flooding will occur
- equipment failures will occur
- telemetry interruptions will occur
- overload conditions will occur

The objective is not perfect uninterrupted operation.

The objective is:

- survivability
- controlled degradation
- operational continuity
- public safety
- environmental stabilization
