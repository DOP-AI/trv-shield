# TRV-SHIELD System Overview

## Mission

TRV-SHIELD is a prevention-first environmental interception and resilience framework designed to protect public health, ecosystems, and coastal infrastructure within the Tijuana River Valley region.

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:

> Dignity Over Power

The system prioritizes:

- prevention over reaction
- transparent operational visibility
- fail-soft infrastructure
- predictive environmental intelligence
- modular resilience engineering
- human-readable oversight

---

# System Purpose

TRV-SHIELD exists to reduce the likelihood and severity of uncontrolled contamination events by intercepting, stabilizing, treating, and monitoring contaminated flows before large-scale environmental and public-health damage occurs.

The framework is designed to support:

- surge-flow interception
- wastewater resilience
- flood-state continuity
- environmental telemetry
- anomaly detection
- operational transparency
- ecosystem protection
- long-term regional resilience
