# Deployment Strategy

## Mission

TRV-SHIELD is designed as a scalable, modular environmental resilience framework capable of phased deployment under real-world operational constraints.

The deployment strategy prioritizes:

- prevention-first infrastructure
- modular scalability
- operational continuity
- public transparency
- flood survivability
- long-term ecosystem stabilization

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:

> Dignity Over Power
