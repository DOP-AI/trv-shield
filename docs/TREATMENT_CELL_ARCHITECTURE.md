# Treatment Cell Architecture

## Mission

TRV-SHIELD treatment systems are designed around modular resilience rather than monolithic dependency.

Instead of relying on a single oversized treatment chain, the system is organized into independent modular treatment cells capable of operating, isolating, and scaling separately.

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:

> Dignity Over Power
